Habesha food — rich and spicy

Habesha food is recognized by many reviewers as rich and spicy with variety of flavors. It shares some features with Asia and the Mediterranean foods. The basic of Habesha food are:

Injera

Injera is a must for every Habesha meal. It is a pancake-like flatbread made mostly from fermented taff/tef.

Berbere

Probably, berebere is what makes Habesha food is unique. It is a mix of more than 20 spices and ingredients with powdered chili peppers.

Mitmita

In short, it is hotter than berbere. It is used to spice up one's plate.

Tesmi/Kibbeh

It is a clarified butter which is mixed with many ingredients and spices.
